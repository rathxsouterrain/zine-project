Quick Info

Welcome to Shopee Flash Sale Bot Dashboard!

This tool helps you automate flash sale purchases on Shopee. Before starting:

  1. Create a session by copying cookies from your logged-in Shopee account
  2. Configure the target product URL and variations
  3. Set up auto-checkout preferences
  4. Start the bot and monitor logs
Note: This bot may not work properly due to Shopee's anti-bot detection. Use at your own risk.
